Two submenu patterns — pick one

A Drop-down (accordion)

The parent stays in the sidebar; clicking it expands the sub-items in place, pushing everything below it down. Best when you want sub-items always visible once opened, and you have room. This is the most common app-sidebar pattern.

→ Click “Fundraising” in the sidebar to expand/collapse it.

B Slide-out (flyout submenu)

The parent reveals a panel that slides out to the right on hover, floating over the content — the sidebar itself never grows. Best for deep menus or to keep the sidebar compact. This is closer to a macOS / command-bar feel.

→ Hover “Raise Toolkit” to see the panel slide out.
