How it works

Readiness in. Capital out.

One platform that scores both sides, then puts them in a live market where activity is visible.

1

Founders get scored

AI reviews your materials and produces a Capital Readiness Score across five diligence dimensions. Close the gaps, climb the score.

2

Investors get matched

Quality-scored investors see only diligence-ready deals, ranked to their thesis — no cold inbound, no noise.

3

The market moves

Indicated interest, reviews, and score changes happen in the open, so both sides see a market that's genuinely alive.
